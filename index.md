# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: March 31, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use the **Financial Calculator** (the "App").

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Information You Provide
The App is designed to function primarily as a tool for financial calculations. We do not require you to create an account or provide personal identification information (such as your name or email address) to use the core features of the App.

### B. Automatically Collected Information
When you use the App, certain information may be collected automatically to ensure technical stability and improved user experience:
*   **Device Information**: We may collect information about your mobile device, such as the hardware model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers (e.g., for ad personalization).
*   **Usage Information**: We may collect anonymous data about how you interact with the App, such as features used and navigation patterns.

## 2. Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

### Google AdMob
We use **Google AdMob** to display advertisements. AdMob may use device identifiers and cookies to serve personalized ads based on your interests. You can learn more about how Google uses data by visiting [Google’s Privacy & Terms](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads).

## 3. How We Use Information
We use the information we collect to:
*   Maintain and optimize the App's performance.
*   Understand usage trends to develop new financial tools.
*   Deliver relevant advertisements through AdMob.

## 4. Data Retention (Offline-First Privacy)
**Privacy is our priority.** The App is built as an **offline-first** tool. All calculation results and history data are stored **locally on your device**. We do not transmit your financial data to our servers. If you delete the App, this local data will be removed.

## 5. Security
We strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting any information automatically collected, but remember that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

## 6. Children's Privacy
These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
